Q_#

Pint Quantity with custom units and additional methods.

Custom units are gC, g_gluc, and g_dw. Custom methods are defined below in this module.

zeta_gluc = <Quantity(0.399666944, 'g_carbon / g_gluc')>#

Grams of carbon per grams glucose.

zeta_dw = <Quantity(0.5, 'g_carbon / g_dw')>#

Grams of carbon per grams dry weight.
