type_aliases#

MMMM

Collection of type aliases used in the project.

Module Attributes

Quantity = <function NewType.<locals>.new_type>#

Pint Quantity type. Displayed as Q_.

TreeSoilInterface#

Interface between a tree and a soil model.

Shape: {tree_pool_from: {soil_pool_to_1: frac1}, …}. See also values of tree_soil_interfaces.

alias of Dict[str, Dict[str, float]]

WoodProductInterface#

Interface between a tree and a wood product model.

See values of wood_product_interfaces.

alias of Dict[str, Any]

InputFluxes#

Fluxes into pools. Key is pool name.

alias of Dict[str, Q_]

OutputFluxesInt#

Fluxes out of pools. Key is pool number not name.

alias of Dict[int, Q_]

TreeExternalOutputFluxes#

External output fluxes from a tree.

External output fluxes from trees.single_tree_C_model.SingleTreeCModel. According to TreeSoilInterface, Stand distributes them to the atmosphere or to the soil or the atmosphere (pool_to=None).

alias of Dict[Tuple[str, Optional[str]], Q_]

CuttingFluxes#

Distribution of tree material at cutting or thinning.

  • (pool_from, pool_to): fraction, pool_to = None means output to atmosphere

alias of Dict[Tuple[str, str], Q_]

OneSpeciesParams#

Parameter dictionary for one species. Examples in single_tree_params.

alias of Dict[str, Any]

SpeciesParams#

Parameter dictionary for all involved tree species.

Example in single_tree_params.

alias of Dict[str, Dict[str, Any]]

MSData#

The tuple is a (Trigger, Action) pair for a MeanTree to plant.

The strings must be found in py.

alias of Tuple[str, str]

MSDataList#

A list of management_strategy_data for a MeanTree to plant.

alias of List[Tuple[str, str]]

SimulationProfile#

Simulation described by a list of trees.

List[Tuple[tree_species, dbh_in_cm, N_in_m2-1, management_strategy_data, waiting or not]]. ‘waiting’ means the MeanTree is not immediately planted at the beginning of the simulation. So it needs some kind of (trigger, plant_action) in the management_strategy_data. It is optional, if omitted, the tree will be planted right away.

alias of List[Tuple[str, float, float, List[Tuple[str, str]], Optional[str]]]

TreeSetting#

A setting of a MeanTree to be planted in the stand. [nr_of_the_tree, dbh_in_cm, N_in_m2-1, management_strategy, ‘waiting’ or omitted].

alias of Tuple[int, Quantity, Quantity, ManagementStrategy, Optional[str]]

SpeciesSettings#

The species setting of a new Stand.

The key is supposed to give the tree species.

alias of Dict[str, List[Tuple[int, Quantity, Quantity, ManagementStrategy, Optional[str]]]]
