class Stand(z, loc, ctr, water_p, soil_p, soil_model, tree_soil_interface, wood_product_model, wood_product_interface)[source]#

Bases: object

A stand is a compilation of trees, a soil model and a wood product model.

It is used as basis to model microclimatic regimes (light, wind), canopy and soil water budget and call tree A-gs computations. Trees return their external output fluxes to the stand who in turn forwards soil inputs to the soil model.

The productivity part of the stand runs of half-hourly basis, whereas the trees and the soil models are updated annualy.

assign_emergency_action(tree_name, emergency_action_str, emergency_direction, emergency_q, nr_of_escalation, logfile_path)[source]#

Decide what to do when a tree cannot sustain itself.

Parameters:

  • tree_name (str) – the name of the MeanTree that failed to be updated

  • emergency_action_str (str) –

    element of [“Cut”, “CutWait3AndReplant”, “Thin”, “Die”]

    • Cut: Cuts a number of trees from above or below

    • CutWait3AndReplant: Like “Cut” but with delayed replanting.

    • Thin: Thin the entire stand equally.

    • Die: Remove the unsustaibale tree.

  • emergency_direction (str) – above or below, applies only for cutting

  • emergency_q (float) – Fraction of trees to keep on Thin

  • nr_of_escalation (int) – If cutting one tree is not enough, cut two, and so on.

  • logfile_path (Path) – path to the logfile
